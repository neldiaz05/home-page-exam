Why Law isCool is Cool

Law isCool is a vibrant online community built by law students, for law students and being extended to bar examinees and aspiring paralegals. We’re a team of aspiring legal professionals who believe that learning law shouldn’t be intimidating or boring—it should be accessible, engaging, and empowering.

We are future lawyers, advocates, and changemakers, united by our passion for legal education and our desire to support fellow students on their law school journey.

Why We Do What We Do

We know what it’s like to struggle with complicated cases, endless readings, and bar exam pressure. That’s why we created Law isCool—a space where law students can find study resources, exchange ideas, prepare for moot courts, and grow their legal minds together.

Our mission is simple:

    • ✅ Make legal learning cool, not cold.
      ✅ Build a supportive law student community.
      ✅ Help future lawyers thrive—not just survive—in law school and beyond.

    • Featuring aspiring Filipino lawyers.

    Here, we learn together. We help each other through tough readings, case digests, recitations, moot courts, and bar exam preparations. We celebrate the wins, and we lift each other up through the struggles.

    Mission Statement

    Law isCool exists to empower the next generation of Filipino legal minds by making legal education accessible, collaborative, and engaging.

    We are committed to creating a supportive learning space where law students, bar examinees, and aspiring paralegals can connect, learn, and thrive together—breaking down the complexities of the law into meaningful, practical knowledge for a just and informed society.
